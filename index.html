LEVANT HISTORY

...a website created for the sake of learning about where
humanity’s first civilization/s started. Learn about every country,
city or state in Levant.

/ˈlɛv.ənt/

“To rise” in French, or “Al-Mashriq” in Arabic is the name given to a group of countries along the eastern Mediterranean shores. Levantine countries have been known to have rich culture and history and this project aims to enrich and remind people of this fact.
